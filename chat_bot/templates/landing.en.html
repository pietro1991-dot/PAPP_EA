The Game-Changer

Why combining several strategies makes risk collapse

PHAI isn't "an EA": it's 5 strategies that earn in 2 opposite, uncorrelated ways — trend and reversion. When one struggles, the other often works: combine them in a package and the drawdown collapses (e.g. a single strategy alone can reach 54%; in the Complete Package the combined drawdown drops to ~10%).

📐

Trend — price lines

On the daily chart it identifies the crossings between price and 8 key lines (Median + 7 moving averages). Only patterns validated out of sample trade. On EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/CHF.

🔄

Reversion — relative value

Measures how much a cross is "stretched" relative to its average and bets on the return (mean-reversion). On EUR/GBP and GBP/CHF — uncorrelated with the trend strategies.

🛡️

Risk first and foremost

Out-of-sample validation (trained up to 2020, tested from 2020 to today) and the most dangerous patterns intentionally disabled. No curve-fitting.
